Speaker Notes
Speaker Notes vs Slide Text ‚Äî and How to Use Both
‚ñº
The #1 mistake in presentations: putting your script on the slides. Speaker notes exist so you can write detailed talking points while keeping slides clean and minimal for the audience.

Key Takeaways

  • Notes panel = below the slide in Normal view; click to expand
  • Slides = what the audience sees. Notes = what you say
  • You can view notes privately during Presenter View while audience sees only slides
  • File ‚Üí Print ‚Üí Print Layout ‚Üí Notes Pages to print with your script
