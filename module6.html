‚ñº
Learn the critical difference between an Appointment (just you) and a Meeting (invites others). Master the correct workflow: title, time, location/Teams link, agenda in the body, add attendees, and Send.

Key Takeaways

Avoid Conflicts with Scheduling Assistant / Find a Time
‚ñº
Use Outlook's built-in Scheduling Assistant (classic) or Find a Time (new Outlook) to view all attendees' calendars at once. Find a conflict-free slot and adjust meeting length when needed.

Key Takeaways

  • Switch to Scheduling Assistant tab after adding attendees
  • Blue = your time blocked; other colors = others' conflicts
  • Use AutoPick / Suggested Times for the fastest conflict-free slot
  • Consider shortening duration if schedules are tight
